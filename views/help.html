Goal

Reveal all squares without an egg to win.

If you breake an egg, you lose the game.

Controls

Desktop
  • Left-click an empty square to reveal it.
  • Right-click a no revealed square to mark it with a flag or a doubt.
  • Double-click a square with the same number than the number of flagged adjacents squares (it can't have a adjacent doubt mark) to reveal the surrounds.
  • Click the smiley face to start a new game.
Phone
  • Tap an empty square to reveal it.
  • Long press (more than one second) a no revealed square to mark it with a flag or a doubt.
  • Tap the smiley face to start a new game.
Based in Minesweeper Online documentation.
